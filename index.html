01 / About

Code, curiosity,
and a love of rhythm.

A brief look at who I am, beyond the CV.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer based in Lisbon with over a decade of experience building frontend applications and content management platforms. My path went from academic research — where I explored music recommendation and interactive visualisation — to leading frontend development on enterprise-grade software.

I believe the best software feels invisible: beautifully crafted, accessible, and a joy to use. Whether it's a complex Angular platform or a carefully tuned Web Component, I bring the same care and attention to detail to every layer of the stack.

Outside the terminal, you'll find me at a concert, on a dance floor, or somewhere new in the world — always chasing that feeling of something alive and in the moment.
