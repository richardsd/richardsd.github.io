Hey — I'm Ricardo

I'm a Senior Software Engineer based in Porto, Portugal, specializing in front-end development with Angular, design systems, and creating delightful user experiences.

With a PhD in Computer Engineering and over a decade of experience, I've worked on everything from enterprise content management platforms to low-code application builders. I care deeply about code quality, testing, performance, and building tools that make developers' lives easier.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me playing guitar, learning piano, practicing Spanish and French, or planning my next trip.

Recent highlights

  • Leading front-end development on the Hyland Experience Platform, building accessible Angular components and design systems used by enterprise customers worldwide.
  • Championing testing practices — introduced comprehensive unit and E2E test coverage (Jasmine, Playwright) and mentored developers on testing best practices.
  • Published research on music recommendation systems and interactive visualization at ACM MM and Springer journals.
